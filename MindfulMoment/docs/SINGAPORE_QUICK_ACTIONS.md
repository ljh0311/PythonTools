# Singapore Quick Actions - Public Awareness System

## Overview

The Singapore Quick Actions feature provides contextually relevant emergency information, safety tips, social guidelines, and mindfulness exercises specifically tailored for Singapore's unique environment, culture, and public transport system.

## Features

### 1. Emergency Information

- **Police**: 999
- **Ambulance & Fire**: 995

- **Tourist Police**: 1800-736-2000

**MRT Emergency Procedures:**

### 2. Safety Tips
**MRT & Public Transport:**
- Stand behind yellow line on platforms
- Let passengers alight before boarding
- Keep phone away when crossing roads near MRT stations
- Be aware of platform gaps
- Hold handrails on escalators and moving walkways

**Walking & Pedestrian Safety:**
- Use pedestrian crossings and overhead bridges
- Wait for green man signal before crossing
- Don't use phone while crossing roads
- Extra caution during rainy weather
- Stay on designated walkways

**Digital Safety:**
- Keep phone in pocket when walking
- Use headphones at moderate volume
- Don't share location publicly on social media
- Be cautious of public WiFi networks
- Keep phone charged for emergencies

**Shopping & Crowded Areas:**
- Keep belongings close in crowded malls
- Be aware of pickpockets in tourist areas
- Don't leave bags unattended
- Use designated smoking areas only
- Follow COVID-19 safety guidelines

### 3. Social Guidelines
**Cultural Etiquette:**
- Greet people with smile and nod
- Use both hands when giving/receiving items
- Remove shoes when entering homes or temples
- Dress modestly when visiting religious sites
- Respect personal space in crowded areas

**Communication:**
- English is widely spoken - feel free to ask for help
- Speak at moderate volume in public spaces
- Be patient with language barriers
- Use "please" and "thank you" frequently
- Listen actively when others are speaking

**Public Interactions:**
- Offer seat to elderly, pregnant, or disabled passengers
- Queue orderly and wait your turn
- Hold doors open for others
- Say "excuse me" when passing through crowds
- Help tourists who look lost or confused

**Building Connections:**
- Join community events and activities
- Volunteer for local causes
- Participate in neighborhood gatherings
- Learn about different cultures in Singapore
- Share your own culture and experiences

### 4. Mindfulness Exercises
**Nature Connection (Garden City Breathing):**
1. Find a green space (parks, gardens, tree-lined streets)
2. Take 5 deep breaths, noticing the fresh air
3. Observe different shades of green around you
4. Listen to birds, wind, and natural sounds
5. Feel gratitude for Singapore's beautiful nature

**Urban Mindfulness (City Observation Walk):**
1. Walk slowly and notice architectural details
2. Count 5 things you can see, hear, smell, and feel
3. Observe the rhythm of the city
4. Notice how different areas have different energies
5. Appreciate the blend of old and new Singapore

**Transport Mindfulness (MRT Journey Awareness):**
1. Notice sounds of train and announcements
2. Feel the movement and rhythm of the journey
3. Observe the diverse people around you
4. Practice patience during delays or crowds
5. Use travel time for reflection, not just phone use

**Food Culture Mindfulness (Hawker Center Awareness):**
1. Notice aromas of different cuisines
2. Observe the skill of food preparation
3. Appreciate the communal dining experience
4. Savor each bite mindfully
5. Express gratitude for the food and culture

**Quick Mindfulness Breaks:**
- **3-Minute Breathing Space**: Notice experience → Focus on breath → Expand awareness
- **Gratitude Practice**: Think of 3 things grateful for in Singapore, notice kindness of strangers, appreciate safety and cleanliness

## Usage Instructions

### Accessing Quick Actions:
1. Navigate to the Public Awareness page
2. Scroll to the "Quick Actions" section
3. Click on any of the four action buttons:
   - **Emergency Info** (Red) - Emergency contacts and procedures
   - **Safety Tips** (Green) - Safety guidelines for various situations
   - **Social Guidelines** (Blue) - Cultural etiquette and social interaction tips
   - **Mindfulness Exercises** (Yellow) - Mindfulness practices for Singapore context

### Modal Navigation:
- **Close Button**: Click the X in the top-right corner
- **Got it Button**: Click to acknowledge and close the modal
- **Scroll**: Use mouse wheel or touch gestures to scroll through content
- **Keyboard**: Use Tab to navigate and Enter/Escape to close

### Language Switching:
1. Use the language selector at the top of the page
2. Choose from English, Chinese, Malay, or Tamil
3. Modal content will update to reflect the selected language
